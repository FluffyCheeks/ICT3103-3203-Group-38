Who are we?

Pastel De Luna is an 831 year old mooncake confectionery business located in the heart of Chinatown. For the past few years, we have seen the impact of COVID-19, which caused physical fairs and our customers to decrease tremendously during the festive season. This affects our businesses' profitability and revenue. Furthermore, physical festive fairs are only held once a year, which makes it difficult to reach out to those who crave our mooncakes after the festival. Hence, to ensure our business sustainability, Pastel De Luna has decided to jump on the digital bandwagon and market our mooncakes online with the aim of bringing the mooncake festive season to life all year long x24/7! Anywhere, Anytime!
